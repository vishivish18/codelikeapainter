Code like a painter

On a sunny morning in August 2010, I started my quest in the computer world,
which was my childhood dream. With experience of zero lines of code under my
belt, I had no experience of what it feels to see your logic do the magic. Since
then I have been fortunate to be surrounded by like minded people and getting
opportunities to learn and grow. I am not a pro, infact I have just begun, but yes
I am having fun. This blog is for sharing my journey through the world of code.
And the blog title is dedicated to Late Dr. Pervez Ahmed, the man who taught us
how to code, like a painter.
